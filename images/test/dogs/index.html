Beagle's

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger
foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting rabbit or hare, known as
beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagle
is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and
foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is a popular pet due to its size
and good temper.
